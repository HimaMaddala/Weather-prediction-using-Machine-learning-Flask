NAIVE BAYES



To make predictions about weather conditions based on historical data which operates on the principles of Bayes' theorem
In the context of weather prediction, the algorithm calculates the probability of a certain weather outcome (like rain, sunny, cloudy,drizzle ) given the observed conditions or features (such as temperature, precipitation, wind speed, etc.). It does this by combining posterior probabilities (probability of certain weather outcomes based on historical data) and likelihoods (how likely certain conditions are given a particular weather outcome). By selecting the weather outcome with the highest probability, Naive Bayes can predict the most probable weather conditions for a given set of observed features.
